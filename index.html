About

Administrative Professional turned software developer, based in Seattle.

My journey in tech consists of growing up in Alaska where I attended the University of Alaska for an Associate's in Computer Information and Office Systems. During this time, I worked for the college and created a student web portal. Intrigued, I began to shift my focus from administration to technology. Upon graduation I decided to continue my education by pursuing a Bachelor's degree in Technology.

Over time my administrative career continued to grow and eventually led to creating wiremocks of a new business the company had opened. I enjoyed the project immensely continued to learn web development in my spare time and began researching options to start a new career in software development. When I found I was accepted into Ada Developers Academy I was ecstatic. I moved across the country and am now starting my journey as a full-stack developer.

When i'm not coding and drinking coffee, I love being outdoors and playing with my dog.
